Our story

We started in a small classroom when we talked about robotics, seems our passions got together by chance. We got to begin to gather information and ask companies until we packed the strength to build a small startup company with lots of connections. Now we teach kids about building robotics and learning their language.

Our vision

We are hoping to get the world to acknowledge that robotics can be the helping hand for our society, doing the hardest tasks efficiently and without time consumption.
